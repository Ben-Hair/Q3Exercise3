Greatest Soccer Players of History

Pictures Name Description World Cups Won
Pele Edson Arantes do Nascimento better known as Pele is considered the greatest players of soccer in history. He currently holds the most wins in the World Cup and had also the world record of scoring the most goals in the sport. Even after retiring from the sport he became the global ambassador for Brazil helping his country score 2 more wins in the World Cup. 3 World Cups
Lionel Messi Lionel Messi is currently regarded as the current best player in soccer today. This became more prevalent after winning the Qatar FIFA World Cup 2022 and was given the title "Player of the Year". 1 World Cup
Diego Maradona Diego Maradona, is an Argentine soccer player, he is best known for his goal named "the Hand of God" where he used his hand to score a goal and stated after "The goal was scored a little bit by the hand of God", hence the name. 1 World Cup
Christiano Ronaldo Christiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese soccer player. Although he hasn't won any World Cups, he is still considered one of the best and most well known soccer players of most time. He is also considered as one of the richest soccer players in the world with a net worth of $490 Million. He is also iconic in the internet for screaming "SIUUUU" which came from Real Madrid when the crowd decided to scream "Si" everytime he scored. He decided to scream the phrase after every goal he had scored ever since. 0 World Cups
